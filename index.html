About Shampoodle


Shampoodle is a full service men and women's hair salon located in downtown Ottawa.

Our professional team is savvy with all the latest styles and trends to provide our customers with their best look. We specialize in classic updos perfect for formal events, single-process colour, highlighting, and perms. Stop by to schedule a consultation or book online today!

We strive for the highest quality of service, while maintaining a fun, friendly, and welcoming environment!

Creative Colour
What is Creative Colour? This is anything that is outside the normal all over base colour or highlights. You’ve heard of some of these terms: Ombre, Sombre, Colour Melting, Dip Dye, vibrant, pastels and more. In these cases, we will consult with each individual client to determine which creative colour technique they need for their desired look. Creative colours involve multiple steps to achieve these looks and can take an unpredictable amount of time. Some may only take an hour before cutting and styling and some may take 2-3 hours. Consultation is key in helping us understand what look you are after.

Cutting and styling costs extra based on your hair length
$65+ per hour
Corrective Colour
Anytime you want to go from a dark base shade to a lighter base shade all over, this is considered a colour correction. We would consider this a corrective colour because it can be unpredictable as to how many steps we may need to take you to the shade that you desire. This process may also result in more than one visit to the salon to keep the integrity of your hair in mind. Corrective colours can be as little as a 1.5-2hour process or can be upwards of 3-4 hours.

Cutting and styling costs extra based on your hair length
$60+ per hour

Meet our Hairdressers!


Meet Beau

Beau has a fresh approach to hairstyling and is fun and easygoing who specializes in kid's cuts.

About Beau

Beau is our newest member to the Shampoodle Stylist Team!

"I've always wanted to be a Hairstylist for as long as I can remember" he says.

When Beau was a child getting his hair cut, he remembers thinking how cool he thought hairstyling was and that he wanted to do it. This dream pushed him to persue this creative career. The more he learned, the more his passion grew.

Meet Diana

Diana is a technical stylist whose inspiration comes from strong shapes and feminine lines.

About Diana

Diana attends many of the top hairshows every year. Attendance at these amazing shows allows her to meet some of the top names in hairstylists today. It also allows her to sample the many different techniques these stylists bring to the world of hair.

Diana is a technical stylist with influence that comes from strong shapes. "I like hair that flows, that is feminine and I cut hair to be very versatile. It's a joy to be a part of clients' lives and to know that you have a hand in helping them feel and look their best!"
